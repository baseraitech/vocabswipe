Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

VocabSwipe ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

VocabSwipe is designed with privacy in mind. We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Learning Progress Data: Your vocabulary review history, word mastery levels, quiz scores, and daily learning streaks are stored locally on your device.
  • User Preferences: Your selected language level (A1-C2), daily goal settings, theme preference, and word type filters.
  • Device Information (via Google Play): If you download from Google Play Store, standard analytics may collect device type, OS version, app version, and crash logs to improve stability.
  • Optional Information: If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address, messages, and any attachments you voluntarily provide.

What We Do NOT Collect: We do not collect your name, precise location, contact list, photos, videos, browsing history, or biometric data unless you explicitly share it.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Personalizing your learning experience and tracking progress
  • Generating customized review sessions and quiz challenges
  • Storing achievement badges and daily goal milestones
  • Responding to support requests and improving App functionality
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or security violations

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party marketers or advertisers.

3. Data Storage and Local Processing

VocabSwipe primarily stores your learning data locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases. This means:

  • Your vocabulary progress stays on your phone or tablet
  • Your data is not automatically synced to our servers (unless you enable cloud backup in future versions)
  • Uninstalling the app will delete locally stored data
  • You have full control over when and how your data is shared

4. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Google Play Store Analytics: When you download VocabSwipe from Google Play, Google collects usage statistics, crash reports, and device information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. VocabSwipe does not integrate third-party analytics libraries beyond what Google Play provides by default.

No Advertising: VocabSwipe does not currently serve advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking networks.

No Social Media Integration: The App does not integrate with Facebook, Twitter, or other social networks for tracking or sharing purposes.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

VocabSwipe is designed for learners of all ages, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent
  • We do not use behavioral advertising or tracking targeting children
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time
  • All data processing is limited to providing the App's educational service

If you believe we have collected information from a child in violation of COPPA, please contact us immediately at the email below.

6. GDPR and Data Subject Rights (EU Users)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request permanent deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt out of certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 10 below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted local storage using SQLite with standard encryption
  • Secure app distribution through official app stores only
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • No transmission of sensitive data over unencrypted channels

While we strive to protect your data, no security system is 100% impenetrable. We recommend using a device PIN or biometric lock to secure your phone.

8. Data Retention

Your learning data is retained as long as you use VocabSwipe and for a reasonable period after account inactivity (if applicable in future versions). When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted. If you request data deletion, we will remove your information within 30 days.

9. Disclosure of Data

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirement: To comply with court orders, law enforcement requests, or legal obligations
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our legal rights, prevent fraud, or ensure app security
  • Service Providers: To trusted vendors who assist us (e.g., cloud hosting, if adopted), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, non-identifiable statistics about app usage with researchers or partners

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vocabswipe.app or baseraitech@gmail.com
  • Mailing Address: Available upon request for GDPR Data Subject Access Requests

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 7-10 business days.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Modified" date. Continued use of VocabSwipe constitutes acceptance of any revised Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via the App or email (if contact information is available).

12. Additional Information

App Store Compliance: VocabSwipe complies with Google Play Store's data privacy and security requirements, including:

  • Transparent disclosure of data collection and use
  • Permission-based access to device features
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Clear options for users to manage their data

Data Processing Agreement: If you represent an organization and need a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for GDPR compliance, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 3, 2026. Your trust is important to us.
