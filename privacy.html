Privacy Policy

How VocabSwipe handles your data.

Your privacy matters. VocabSwipe is designed to collect only the information needed to improve your learning experience while keeping your data safe and private.

Data We Collect

VocabSwipe stores only the vocabulary progress and learning preferences required to personalize your study routine. We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or location unless you explicitly provide them.

How We Use Data

Progress data is used to generate review sessions, track your daily goals, and unlock achievements. This information stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

VocabSwipe does not integrate with any analytics or advertising platforms by default. If you download the app from Google Play, standard store listing services may collect usage statistics independently.

Security

We use local storage and SQLite management to keep your data safe. We recommend using secure device settings and screen locks to prevent unauthorized access.
